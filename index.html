Curating Online

Visual design and web development for a collaborative digital platform

Curating Online is a digital platform for collective engagement in curatorial practice, led by art historian and curator Marialaura Ghidini. In 2021, I joined the project as a visual designer and developer, supporting the evolution of its visual identity and creating a fully responsive WordPress site. Working closely with the founder, I translated her creative direction into visual assets and built a clean, intuitive platform for long-term growth.


Project Overview

The platform was conceived as a digital hub to explore curating as a process of care, research, and shared experience. My goal was to provide aesthetic coherence, structural clarity, and a flexible system that could evolve with future content and curatorial projects. Based on direction from Marialaura Ghidini, I produced graphic assets, supported layout and interaction decisions, and implemented the site on WordPress with a modular backend structure.

Goals & Challenges

  • Translate a conceptual vision into a functional, elegant digital platform
  • Design visual elements and layouts aligned with curatorial sensibilities
  • Build a WordPress site that is easy to update and expand over time
  • Balance minimalism with warmth, accessibility, and artistic personality

My Role

I collaborated as a visual designer and developer, focusing on the implementation of Marialaura Ghidini’s creative vision:

  • Created typographic and layout proposals using Figma
  • Developed illustrations and graphic components to enhance identity
  • Built and customized the WordPress site using modular page templates
  • Advised on structural and aesthetic decisions throughout the process

Process Highlights

1. Visual Discovery & Figma Assets

Based on the client’s references and themes, I explored layouts, colors and type systems in Figma, sharing annotated files for review and iteration.

2. WordPress Development

Using Elementor and custom CSS, I built reusable templates and interactive sections that reflect the platform's curatorial nature and evolving content structure.

3. Design Consulting

Throughout the collaboration, I offered strategic advice on how to align technical possibilities with curatorial needs, ensuring that visual and functional choices enhanced the visitor’s experience.

Results & Impact

The site became a functional home for curatorial essays, invitations to contribute, and experimental projects. The identity balances minimalism with character, and the structure is equipped to scale with future initiatives.

Key Outcomes:

  • Delivered a fully functional, scalable WordPress site with modular templates
  • Enhanced brand identity through typography, layout and soft interactivity
  • Enabled curators to manage and expand content with ease
  • Contributed to a visual framework that supports experimental online curating
